EXPERTSHUB SAFETY GUIDELINES - WORKER

Purpose:
These guidelines protect both the worker and the customer during in-person service visits.
Follow them before, during, and after every booking.

1) BEFORE LEAVING FOR THE JOB
- Review booking details fully (address, service type, scheduled time, special notes).
- Confirm customer contact details only through the app.
- Keep your phone charged and internet active.
- Share trip details with a trusted person for long-distance or late-hour jobs.
- Do not accept bookings outside the ExpertsHub app workflow.

2) ARRIVAL AND IDENTITY CHECK
- Meet only at the booking address shown in the app.
- Greet professionally and verify customer name and booking ID.
- Never start work if booking details do not match the person or location.
- If something feels suspicious, pause and contact support.

3) OTP AND START-OF-WORK RULES
- Start work only after customer gives START OTP in person.
- Never ask for OTP over phone/chat before physical verification.
- Upload clear BEFORE photos as required.
- Keep location sharing active during CONFIRMED/IN_PROGRESS service.

4) PERSONAL AND PROFESSIONAL CONDUCT
- Maintain respectful communication at all times.
- Keep all conversation related to the job and within app chat/calls.
- Do not request personal favors, private meetings, or unrelated services.
- Do not consume alcohol/substances before or during service.

5) MONEY AND PAYMENT SAFETY
- Accept payment only through ExpertsHub-approved payment flow.
- Never request direct bank transfer, UPI to personal account, or cash outside policy.
- Do not share bank PIN, OTP, or wallet credentials with anyone.

6) DURING SERVICE
- Work only in approved service areas and scope.
- Keep expensive tools/parts secured and monitored.
- Inform the customer before extra work, cost changes, or delays.
- If any unsafe behavior occurs, stop work and move to a safe place.

7) EMERGENCY AND THREAT RESPONSE
- Use SOS immediately if there is danger, harassment, violence, or forced confinement.
- Move to a public/safe location as soon as possible.
- Call emergency services if urgent medical or physical threat exists.
- Notify ExpertsHub support with booking ID and incident details.

8) INCIDENT REPORTING
- Use Report option in the booking for harassment, fraud, threats, no-show issues, or disputes.
- Submit factual details: time, location, what happened, witnesses, and evidence links.
- Avoid emotional or abusive language in reports; keep it precise and verifiable.

9) COMPLETION RULES
- Finish work according to agreed scope.
- Upload AFTER photos where required.
- Ask customer for COMPLETION OTP only after service is fully done.
- Never pressure customer for OTP before completion.

10) DATA AND PRIVACY
- Never copy customer personal data for personal use.
- Do not store customer photos/documents outside approved workflow.
- Do not share customer address/phone details with third parties.

11) WHAT TO NEVER DO
- Never work without OTP verification.
- Never take off-platform payments.
- Never threaten, abuse, discriminate, or retaliate against customers.
- Never share account credentials, OTPs, or internal support messages.

12) QUICK SAFETY CHECKLIST (WORKER)
- Booking verified
- Customer identity confirmed
- START OTP received in person
- BEFORE photo uploaded
- Location sharing active
- In-app communication only
- COMPLETION OTP requested only after full completion
- Report/SOS used immediately if needed

If in doubt, prioritize safety first. Stop work, secure yourself, and contact support.
