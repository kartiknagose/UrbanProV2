EXPERTSHUB SAFETY GUIDELINES - CUSTOMER

Purpose:
These guidelines protect both the customer and the worker during in-person service visits.
Follow them before, during, and after every booking.

1) BEFORE THE VISIT
- Book services only through ExpertsHub.
- Verify service details, date, time, and address in booking.
- Keep your phone charged and internet active.
- Inform a family member/friend if service is at odd hours.
- Set up emergency contacts in Safety Settings.

2) WHEN THE WORKER ARRIVES
- Verify worker name/profile and booking details in app before entry.
- Avoid allowing unknown persons not linked to booking.
- Keep the service area visible and reasonably supervised.
- If identity does not match app details, do not proceed and contact support.

3) OTP SAFETY RULES
- Share START OTP only after worker arrives and identity is verified.
- Never share OTP over call/chat before arrival.
- Share COMPLETION OTP only after job is fully completed to your satisfaction.
- Never share OTP under pressure.

4) COMMUNICATION AND BOUNDARIES
- Keep communication job-related and within app chat/calls.
- Do not share unnecessary personal or financial details.
- Keep interactions professional and respectful.
- If you feel uncomfortable at any point, pause the service.

5) PAYMENT SAFETY
- Pay only through ExpertsHub payment flow.
- Do not pay to personal UPI/QR/bank accounts unless explicitly approved by platform policy.
- Do not share card OTP, UPI PIN, CVV, or bank passwords with anyone.

6) DURING SERVICE
- Ensure children/elders are safe and away from risky tools/chemicals.
- Keep valuables secured.
- Confirm additional costs or scope changes in app before approval.
- Report unusual behavior immediately.

7) EMERGENCY RESPONSE
- Use SOS immediately for danger, harassment, threats, or violence.
- Move yourself and family members to a safe place first.
- Contact emergency services for urgent medical/physical danger.
- Inform ExpertsHub support with booking ID and quick facts.

8) REPORTING ISSUES
- Use Report option for misconduct, harassment, fraud, damage, no-show, or payment disputes.
- Provide clear details: time, actions, losses, and evidence links.
- Submit reports as soon as possible while facts are fresh.

9) AFTER SERVICE
- Inspect service quality before sharing COMPLETION OTP.
- Keep invoice/payment records in app.
- Submit an honest rating and review to help platform safety.

10) PRIVACY AND SECURITY
- Do not share home entry codes, private cameras, or personal documents unnecessarily.
- Do not disclose account passwords or OTPs.
- Avoid sharing sensitive family routines/schedules with unknown persons.

11) WHAT TO NEVER DO
- Never share START OTP before worker arrives.
- Never share COMPLETION OTP before job is complete.
- Never make off-platform payments under pressure.
- Never ignore threatening or suspicious behavior.

12) QUICK SAFETY CHECKLIST (CUSTOMER)
- Worker identity verified in app
- START OTP shared only after arrival verification
- In-app communication maintained
- Payment kept on platform
- COMPLETION OTP shared only after full job completion
- Emergency contacts configured
- SOS/Report used immediately when needed

If in doubt, prioritize safety first. Pause the job and contact support.
