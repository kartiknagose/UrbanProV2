DEPLOYMENT STEPS:
1. Go to Render dashboard → Web Service → Settings
2. Scroll to "Environment Variables"
3. Click "Add Environment Variable"
4. Replace placeholders:
   - [YOUR-FRONTEND-DOMAIN] → your Vercel URL
   - [COPY-FROM-SUPABASE-DASHBOARD...] → get from Supabase Settings→API
   - [SET-A-SECRET-TOKEN...] → create a random secret for email health probe
5. Paste each line into the key=value format
6. Click "Save Changes"
7. Redeploy service

VERIFICATION POST-DEPLOY:
- Backend health: curl https://your-render-domain/health
- Email check: curl -H "x-email-health-token: YOUR-TOKEN" https://your-render-domain/health/email
- Test email send: curl -X POST -H "x-email-health-token: YOUR-TOKEN" https://your-render-domain/health/email/send-test?to=your-email
